Methodology notes
  • Lookback: 2 years of daily closing prices from yfinance.
  • Returns: Simple daily returns (close-to-close).
  • Beta: Cov(stock, SPY) / Var(SPY) over the full 2-year window.
  • Annualised volatility: Std(daily returns) × √252.
  • Max drawdown: Worst peak-to-trough decline in the 2-year window.
  • Sharpe-like ratio: (annualised return − 4.5% risk-free) / annualised vol. Uses a 4.5% risk-free proxy (approximate T-bill yield).
  • Correlation: Pearson correlation of daily returns vs SPY.
  • Limitations: Backward-looking only; survivorship bias applies. Vol and drawdown regimes change. Not a predictor of future risk.
