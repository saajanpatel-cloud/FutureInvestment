Methodology notes
  • EPS-based DCF: Forward EPS (analyst consensus NTM) projected at growth rate for 5 years. Each year's earnings discounted at WACC. Terminal value via Gordon growth model (g = 2.5%).
  • Revenue-based DCF: For pre-profit names (metric=ps), revenue per share is projected forward then valued at a terminal EV/Revenue multiple of 3x, discounted back.
  • Projection period: 5 years.
  • Growth range: Bear CAGR to Bull CAGR from scenario_assumptions.csv with 3 intermediates.
  • Discount rates: 8%, 9%, 10%, 11%, 12% — one column per WACC in the grid.
  • Limitations: Single-metric model; ignores buybacks, dilution, dividends, capex, and balance-sheet changes. Terminal value dominates in most cells — treat as directional, not precise.
